Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know before joining DevConf 2026.

Who is DevConf 2026 for?

DevConf 2026 is designed for software engineers, developers, founders, students, and technology enthusiasts who want to learn, connect, and explore the future of software development.

Where and when will DevConf 2026 take place?

DevConf 2026 is a three-day conference bringing together developers from around the world for technical talks, workshops, and meaningful networking opportunities.

Are workshops included with my ticket?

Standard tickets include access to two workshop sessions, while Pro and Team attendees receive unlimited workshop access throughout the conference.

Will conference sessions be recorded?

Yes. Standard attendees receive access to session recordings for 30 days, while Pro and Team ticket holders receive lifetime access.

Can I attend DevConf as a student or beginner?

Absolutely. DevConf welcomes developers at every experience level. Sessions range from accessible introductions to advanced technical deep dives, so there is something valuable for everyone.

Can I get a refund if my plans change?

Tickets can be refunded according to the conference cancellation policy. You can also transfer your ticket to another attendee before the event begins.
